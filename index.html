Sammie Lee

Also Known As

DJ World Lee & Jammin Lee

Sammie Lee is a premier DJ, remixer, producer and all around digital musician. Started in Chicagos’ House Music scene while signed with Code 3 Records at age 14. Later in his career he signed with Trax Records, also founded two bands ‘Internal Sanctuary” and “Endiskize”. He is also a band memeber with Method of Control.
